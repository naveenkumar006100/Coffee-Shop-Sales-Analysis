# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Analysis Dashboard

An interactive **Coffee Shop Sales Analysis Dashboard** built using **Microsoft Excel** to analyze sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, product performance, and store-wise revenue. This project leverages **Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, KPI Cards, and Slicers** to transform raw transactional data into meaningful business insights.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

The objective of this project is to analyze coffee shop sales data and create an interactive dashboard that enables business stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify sales trends, evaluate product performance, and support data-driven decision-making.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance across different store locations, product categories, order sizes, days of the week, and hours of the day.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance.
- Monitor total revenue and transaction volume.
- Identify best-selling product categories and product types.
- Compare performance across store locations.
- Analyze customer ordering behavior by hour.
- Evaluate sales trends by day and month.
- Support business decisions using interactive visualizations.

# 📊 Dashboard Analysis

## 1. Total Sales

The dashboard reports an overall revenue of **$698,812.33**, representing the total income generated from coffee shop sales.

---

## 2. Customer Transactions

A total of **149,116** transactions were recorded during the analysis period.

---

## 3. Average Customer Spending

Customers spend an average of **$4.69** per transaction.

---

## 4. Average Items Purchased

Each customer purchases an average of **1.44 items** per transaction.

---

## 5. Hourly Sales Trend

The hourly sales chart shows that customer activity is highest during the morning hours, indicating peak business periods.

---

## 6. Product Category Performance

Coffee contributes the largest percentage of total revenue, followed by Bakery products and Drinking Chocolate.

---

## 7. Store Performance

The dashboard compares the performance of three store locations:

- Astoria
- Hell's Kitchen
- Lower Manhattan

This comparison helps identify high-performing and low-performing stores.

---

## 8. Product Performance

The dashboard highlights the Top 5 best-selling products based on revenue, helping management focus on inventory planning and promotions.

---

## 9. Sales by Day

Sales are analyzed across all days of the week to identify high-performing and low-performing business days.

---

# 💡 Key Insights

- Total Revenue reached **$698,812.33**.
- Over **149,000 transactions** were completed.
- Coffee is the highest revenue-generating category.
- Morning hours receive the highest customer traffic.
- Large-sized beverages are the most frequently ordered.
- Barista Espresso is the top-selling product.
- Hell's Kitchen and Astoria are the highest-performing store locations.
- Monday and Friday generate comparatively higher sales.

---

# 🚀 Business Recommendations

- Increase staffing during peak morning hours.
- Maintain adequate inventory for best-selling products.
- Introduce promotional offers for low-performing product categories.
- Launch weekend discounts to improve sales.
- Expand successful sales strategies across all store locations.
- Continuously monitor KPIs for better decision-making.
